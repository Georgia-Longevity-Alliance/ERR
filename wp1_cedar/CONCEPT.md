## Hypothesis

CEDAR (Centriolar Damage Accumulation Theory of Aging) — theory positing that centrioles accumulate irreversible oxidative damage and serve as molecular counters of stem cell divisions. Asymmetric inheritance of "old" centrioles leads to stem cell pool exhaustion.
